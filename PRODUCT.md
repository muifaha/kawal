# Product

## Register

product

## Users
School staff, including Bimbingan Konseling (BK) counselors, Homeroom Teachers (Wali Kelas), Vice Principals (Waka), and Teachers. They use the platform during school hours, often requiring quick access to record attendance, check student behavior history, log violations/remissions, or handle student summons.

## Product Purpose
Kawal is a student attendance and discipline monitoring system. It serves to centralize student data, track attendance status, record disciplinary infractions and remissions via a point system, trigger notifications/summons at specific thresholds (25, 50, 75 points), and document counselor interventions. The goal is to make school discipline management transparent, efficient, and supportive.

## Brand Personality
Professional, trustworthy, and efficient. The tone is administrative and reliable, optimized for high usability and clarity, avoiding unnecessary decorative elements.

## Anti-references
- Cartoonish or overly playful UI patterns that compromise professional authority.
- Cluttered, legacy enterprise layouts typical of old school-management software.
- Generic SaaS "AI slop" templates (e.g., saturated warm cream backgrounds, excessive cards, and arbitrary purple-to-blue gradients).

## Design Principles
1. **Utility & High Density**: Layouts should prioritize information density and fast scanning over decorative whitespace. Data entry and recording flows (like attendance sheet grids) must be extremely streamlined.
2. **Actionable Status**: Thresholds and critical statuses (e.g., student reaching a warning level) should be immediately noticeable and guide the user toward the next step (e.g., printing a summons or calling a meeting).
3. **Calm Institutional Trust**: Use clean borders, clear grids, and professional color coding. The visual system should feel stable, secure, and authoritative.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Standard WCAG AA compliance (minimum contrast ratios of 4.5:1 for body copy).
- Clear, distinct focus states for keyboard navigation.
- High legibility on mobile viewports, as teachers and counselors often access the tool on-the-go.
